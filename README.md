## 📚 Contents

- **ISO Standards**
  - ISO 9001 – Quality Management

- **NIST Framework**
  - NIST 800-53 – Security and Privacy Controls
  - NIST 800-171 – Controlled Unclassified Information

- **FDA Validation**
  - 21 CFR Part 11 – Electronic Records & Signatures

- **Learning Resources**
